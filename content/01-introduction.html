There are several reasons for you to have considered reading this document:

  1. You want to set up your own temporary information screen, for instance, at a conference,
  2. You are a developer who wants to write his/her own Turtle/pane,
  3. You want to set up a managed display with real-time interaction,
  4. You want to use the entire architecture to its full potential, installing a full digital signage platform in the cloud.

This document will go from the first to the fourth step. For each step you will have to understand the previous one.
